Dell Poweredge R440 Rack Server

Introduction

The PowerEdge T340 tower server is a scalable, secure, and easy-to-manage one-socket server. You can easily share and communicate through your company with the PowerEdge T340 to drive business performance.

Features

The PowerEdge T340 is a dependable server that lets you collaborate and expand as your business grows. It aids in the advancement of your company through improved results. It has a lot of memory and redundant power supplies, so it's very reliable.

The T340 includes an integrated iDRAC with Lifecycle Controller that provides agent-free management out of the box and intelligently delivers routine tasks. T340 incorporates new security features to ensure that the right data is delivered safely and efficiently, ensuring loyalty and delivering worry-free, stable systems.
