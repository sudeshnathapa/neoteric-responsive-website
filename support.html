Maintenance Support services

For maintenance support services on client computing products and solutions, a dedicated service center is serving the customers. The customers can create service ticket on their own and track the maintenance progress.

Neoteric’s F1 Service Centre provides ISO-certified authorized service and support for products sold by Neoteric, in warranty and afterwards. In-store Expert Stations offer immediate assistance and support as well.
